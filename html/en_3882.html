Arrow of light Hiroshima

  • 5
  • 0
onomichiphotografar
A liner connecting the islands of Setouchi arrived at Onomichi Port.
It's like a fired arrow.
Illumination of the shipyard's cranes, which are lit on weekends and public holidays, creates the night of Onomichi Suido.
